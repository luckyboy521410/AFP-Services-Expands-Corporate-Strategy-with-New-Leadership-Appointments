AFP-Services, a subsidiary of AFP, is expanding its corporate business development efforts with the appointment of Otman Meriche as director and Jon Dillon as managing director. These leadership changes align with AFP's revitalization plan, aiming to acquire 1,000 new clients over the next five years, particularly in the corporate sector. AFP-Services offers on-demand production services, including video, photo, and reporting, tailored to meet client needs for high-quality content. With dedicated teams and a focus on reliability and innovation, AFP-Services continues to deliver exceptional services in the fast-evolving world of Web Development and multimedia content.
